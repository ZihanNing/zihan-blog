  - Proposed a practical framework for translating offline MR reconstructions to inline deployment, built on the Gadgetron platform ([Project open-sourced](https://github.com/ZihanNing/Practical_Inline_Recon_Framework-public.git) with [detailed instructions](https://github.com/ZihanNing/Practical_Inline_Recon_Framework-public/wiki), and [demo datasets for testing](https://zenodo.org/records/15839027))

- **Head Motion Correction (Inline) Reconstruction [open-sourced]**                   
  **[Principle]** *Sep.2023 ‚Äì Present  üìç King‚Äôs College London & St. Thomas Hospital, UK* 
  - Implemented AlignedSENSE with DISORDER trajectory to several clinical sequences (MPRAGE, MP2RAGE, FLAIR, SWI, etc) to achieve inline head motion correction reconstruction. 
  - Reconstruction [open-sourced](https://github.com/ZihanNing/Practical_Inline_Recon_Framework-public/tree/main/AlignSENSE_Recon_Demo), and validated to adults & neonates. 

  Related publications: [[ISMRM 2025 abstract](/files/Neonate_moco_ISMRM2025.pdf)]; 

- **Large-Scale Cohort Study (Twins UK with UK Biobank) Protocol Development**

- **Advanced Renal Perfusion and Arterial Imaging**  
  **[Principle]** *Apr.2019 ‚Äì Jun.2023  üìç Tsinghua University, China*
  - Proposed the Time-encoded Arterial Spin labeling to cover the whole kidney (TASK) MR technique to achieve multiple time-points renal perfusion measurement in a single 5-min scan. [Paper to be submitted]
  - Proposed a free-breathing simultaneous multi-delay arterial spin labeling and T1 mapping technique (SAMURAI) with a stepwise kinetic model for renal assessment in a single 4-min scan; further implementation of the technique on higher field strength (5T). [[Paper](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/mrm.29268)]
  - Proposed a free-breathing 3D isotropic-resolution MR sequence for simultaneous vessel wall imaging of bilateral renal arteries and adjacent abdominal aorta. [[Paper](https://aapm.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/mp.15436)]
  - Evaluated the feasibility and reproducibility of 2D and 3D black-blood sequences in measuring morphology of renal arterial wall. [[Paper](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0730725X21000254?via%3Dihub)]
   
- **Cerebral  and Carotid Vessel Wall Imaging & Brain Perfusion**  
**[Principle]** *Dec.2018 ‚Äì Jan.2025  üìç Tsinghua University, China*
  - Histologically validated the accuracy of 3D multi-contrast MR cerebral and carotid vessel wall imaging in evaluating carotid vulnerable plaques in patients with carotid moderate-to-severe stenosis. [[Paper](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1097664724011396?via%3Dihub)]
  - Investigated the association between cerebral blood flow and the progression of white matter hyperintensity at a 2-year follow-up of community-based, asymptomatic adults in a longitudinal cohort study across the lifespan. [[Paper](https://qims.amegroups.org/article/view/96613/html)]
